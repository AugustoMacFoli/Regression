Support Vector Regression (SVR)

SVR is an regression algorithm used to work with continuous values.

In simple regression the goal is to minimise the error rate. SVR tries to fit a line to data by minimising a cost function. However, the interesting part about SVR is that it can be deployed with a non-linear kernel. In this case ending making non-linear regression, i.e. fitting a curve rather than a line.

This example shows the relation between the years of experience and salary of employees at a company.
